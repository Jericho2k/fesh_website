Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Welcome to FESH ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our fashion discovery app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using FESH, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

1. Personal Information

  • Name: We collect your name to create and personalize your user profile, authenticate your account, and provide a personalized experience within the app.
  • Email Address: We collect your email address for account registration, authentication, password recovery, sending notifications, and marketing communications (with your consent).

2. User Content

  • Photos and Videos: You may upload photos and videos of clothing items, outfits, and your personal style. This content is used to enable core app functionality, provide AI-powered features (such as image processing and outfit generation), personalize your feed, and analyze content performance.
  • Messages: If you send messages or communicate with other users through the app, we collect and process these communications to enable messaging functionality and improve user experience.

3. Usage Data

  • Product Interaction: We collect data about how you interact with the app, including swipes, likes, saves, views, searches, and other actions. This information is used for analytics, product personalization (to improve recommendations), and core app functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

App Functionality

  • To create and manage your user account
  • To authenticate and secure your account
  • To enable core features such as posting content, saving items, and creating boards
  • To facilitate communication between users
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security
  • To provide customer support

Product Personalization

  • To provide AI-powered recommendations based on your preferences
  • To personalize your feed with content relevant to your interests
  • To generate outfit suggestions and style recommendations
  • To customize your app experience

Analytics

  • To understand how users interact with our app
  • To measure the effectiveness of features
  • To analyze content performance (views, likes, saves)
  • To plan and develop new features
  • To improve app performance and user experience

Marketing and Communications

  • To send you marketing communications about new features, updates, and promotions (with your consent)
  • To notify you about activity on your account (likes, follows, new items from creators you follow)

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (such as hosting, analytics, and customer support).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information with your explicit consent.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access and review your personal information through the app settings.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information at any time.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-out: You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format.

Children's Privacy

FESH is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using FESH, you consent to such transfers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us
